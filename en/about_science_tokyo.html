About Institute of Science Tokyo

A guide to the university our laboratory (Department of Biochemical Pathophysiology / Department of Lipid Biology) belongs to. Despite the word "Institute" in its name, Institute of Science Tokyo is a comprehensive national university.

← Back to home

Institute of Science Tokyo (brand name: Science Tokyo) is a national university established on October 1, 2024 through the merger of Tokyo Medical and Dental University (TMDU) and the Tokyo Institute of Technology (Tokyo Tech).

About the word “Institute”

Although the English name contains the word "Institute," it does not refer to a single research institute. Institute of Science Tokyo is a comprehensive national university spanning medicine, dentistry, and the life sciences through to science and engineering. A major strength is that it combines the medical fields (formerly Tokyo Medical and Dental University) with the science and engineering fields (formerly the Tokyo Institute of Technology).

Our laboratory's affiliation

Our laboratory belongs to the following two organizations within Institute of Science Tokyo:

  • Institute of Integrated Research → Medical Research Laboratory (MRL) → Department of Biochemical Pathophysiology
  • Graduate School of Medical and Dental Sciences → Department of Lipid Biology
